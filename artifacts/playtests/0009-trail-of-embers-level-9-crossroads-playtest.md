# Trail of Embers Level 9 Crossroads Playtest

Date: 2026-07-08
Build committed: `cecb999` — Add Trail of Embers Level 9 crossroads probe
Tester: Ian Chia
Prototype: Trail of Embers

## Summary

- Level 9 began as a “shortcut/commitment” concept, but browser playtesting found it too easy and empty; the shortcut did not feel useful.
- Reverse one-way collision initially appeared broken. A sweep-based collision fix stopped reverse and diagonal slipping.
- The design pivoted from a useful shortcut to a low-stakes wrong-turn lesson.
- “The Crossroads” now teaches route memory through a tempting wrong path: the player wastes time, loops back, and learns not to take it next time.
- Arrow readability and orientation were major iteration issues and required follow-up fixes.
- Final browser check: fixed and committed.

## Remaining Question

Are One-Way Passages worth carrying forward as route-memory friction, rather than as a high-pressure shortcut mechanic?

## Status

This note is playtest evidence, not source-of-truth ratification.
