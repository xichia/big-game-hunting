# First Run Plan

This is the recommended first operating pass for Big Game Hunting.

## Step 1: Broad Game Shape Research

Use `templates/research-artifact.md` to generate a broad research artifact covering possible game shapes.

Suggested prompt:

```text
Produce a DRAFT ONLY research artifact for Big Game Hunting.

Question:
What game shapes are most likely to satisfy simple entry, wide appeal, hard mastery, strategic depth, strong replayability, and low adoption friction?

Explore at least eight directions:
- hidden-information pursuit
- asymmetric predator/prey
- push-your-luck resource risk
- social deduction expedition
- map-control trophy economy
- cooperative hunt with competitive scoring
- simultaneous planning tactics
- party strategy bluffing

For each, evaluate beginner accessibility, mastery ceiling, replayability, social appeal, production complexity, smallest useful probe, and likely failure mode.

Do not update source-of-truth files.
```

## Step 2: Synthesis Packet

Use `templates/synthesis-packet.md` to narrow broad research into 3–5 candidates.

## Step 3: Human Gate

Select candidates for focused research.

## Step 4: Focused Research and Probe

Create a focused feasibility packet and a small probe brief for one selected candidate.

## Step 5: Independent Review

Use `templates/independent-review.md` to evaluate the probe.

## Step 6: Decision Dashboard

Use `templates/decision-dashboard.md` to prepare human ratification.

## Step 7: Source-of-Truth Update

Only after ratification, update `GAME_TRUTH.md`, `MECHANICS.md`, or other source-of-truth files.
