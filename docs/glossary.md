# Glossary

## Artifact

A bounded output produced by a model or human for a specific purpose.

## Source of Truth

The authoritative project state. In Big Game Hunting, this includes `GAME_TRUTH.md`, `DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md`, `MECHANICS.md`, and `DECISION_LOG.md`.

## Ratification

A human decision that allows an artifact or recommendation to update the source of truth.

## Probe

A small bounded test of a mechanic, assumption, loop, or player experience.

## Fable Five

A late-stage specialist synthesis or review pass used after research, gating, and human direction have bounded the brief.

## Worker Model Pool

A set of models assigned to narrow execution tasks. Worker models should not make source-of-truth decisions.

## Game Shape

The broad form of the game: tabletop, digital, party, tactics, negotiation, deduction, asymmetric, cooperative, competitive, or hybrid.

## Core Loop

The repeatable player action cycle that drives the game.

## Mastery Ceiling

The amount of room the game gives players to improve over time.

## Adoption Friction

The difficulty of getting new players to understand, start, enjoy, and teach the game.
